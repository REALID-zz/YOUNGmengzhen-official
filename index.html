孟臻（DCT）

I translate philosophical emotions through a distinct naïve gaze, focusing on painting and contemporary art. Working primarily in oil, I build narrative spaces with sharp chiaroscuro and atmosphere—placing emotion and story side by side for an experience that is both intuitive and reflective.

About

以油画为核心媒介，在强烈明暗对比与氛围建构中搭建叙事空间；以“天真视角”触及哲学情感，让情绪与故事并置，形成兼具直觉与思辨的观看体验。
